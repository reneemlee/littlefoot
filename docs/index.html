LittleFoot Baby

Daily activities for your baby's first year

LittleFoot Baby gives you one simple activity each day to bond with your baby during their first year. From tummy time games to sensory play, each activity is age-appropriate and designed to support your little one's development.

  • A unique daily activity tailored to your baby's age
  • Beautiful hand-drawn illustrations for every activity
  • Browse past activities with the built-in calendar
  • Daily reminder notifications so you never miss a day
  • Track your baby's journey from day 1 to day 365
