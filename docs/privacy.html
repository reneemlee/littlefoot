Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Overview

LittleFoot Baby ("the App") is built with your privacy in mind. We believe your family's data belongs to you and you alone.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data you enter (such as your baby's name and birthday) is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage and is never shared with us or any third parties.

Analytics

The App does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your usage or behavior in any way.

Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications to remind you of daily activities. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings or within the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The Community feature opens an external link to a Facebook group, which is governed by Facebook's own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

While the App is designed for parents of young children, it does not collect any information from or about children. All data remains on the parent's device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at renee@withlychee.com.
